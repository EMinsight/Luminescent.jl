Overview

Generative design meets Maxwell's Equations. GPU and automatic differentiation (AD) compatible FDTD package in Julia for inverse design & topology optimization in semiconductor photonics, acoustics and RF. Uses AD by Zygote.jl for adjoint optimization. Integrates with Jello.jl to generate length scale controlled paramaterized geometry . Staggered Yee grid update with fully featured boundary conditions & sources in 1d/2d/3d.
