Overview

Generative design meets Maxwell's Equations. Differentiable FDTD package for inverse design & topology optimization in semiconductor photonics, acoustics and RF. GPU and automatic differentiation (AD) compatible. Uses AD by Zygote.jl for adjoint optimization. Integrates with Jello.jl to generate length scale controlled paramaterized geometry . Staggered Yee grid update with fully featured boundary conditions & sources in 1d/2d/3d. Customizable physics to potentially incorporate dynamics like heat transfer, charge transport.
